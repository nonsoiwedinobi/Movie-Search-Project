Welcome to What2Watch

We are glad you're here! On What2Watch you are able to find out where to watch your favorite movies & TV series. What2Watch is easy and effective: select your favorite streaming providers in the SearchBar below and see what’s new, hot and trending. Use our filters to find the best movie to stream tonight. Either you’re a fan of horror movies or romantic comedies, or kids movie or series to enjoy with your children, our filters let you browse and easily find a specific movie or series. We also offer news services and you can get current news ranging from entertainment to politics, sports etc. Find out more about the team behind What2Watch here.
